About Us


SAMA PETROLEUM CO.LTD was rstablishedin Buea, Cameroon with the
vision of becoming a trusted supplier and distributor of high-quality
petroleum products and energy solutions across the country


From its beginnings as a regional fuel distribution company. the
business steadily expanded its operations due to increasing demand for
reliable petroleum services within both the commercial and industrial
sectors.


Today, the company operates its head office in Buea with active branches
in Bamenda, Yaounde, and Douala . allowing it to efficiently serve customers
nationwide.
